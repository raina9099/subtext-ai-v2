Subtext AI

AI That Reads Between the Lines

About

Unleashing the power of common-sense AI to decode hidden meanings, subtle sentiments, and complex human expressions in visual and textual content.

Features

Context-Aware Multimodal Understanding

  • Analyzes text, images, and metadata together for richer interpretation.
  • Incorporates cultural and linguistic subtleties for globally relevant insights.

Nuanced Sentiment Analysis

  • Detects complex emotions like sarcasm, irony, humor, and passive-aggressiveness.
  • Provides sentiment scores and detailed context for each piece of content.
